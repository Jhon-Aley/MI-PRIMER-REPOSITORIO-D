The Volunteer

Whats this about?

Long story short, The Volunteer is a school project for the videogames specialization, taking into account that:

  • It has to be an enviromentaly based game
  • Must create awareness about the topic
  • Can be shown directly or indirectly

Those it have a plot?

For this we'er gonna take into account:

      A trash island floating between two conflicted countries, towards a natrual reserve of one of them
      The synthesis and invention of a trash decomposing bacteria
      The persecution of scientist around the world
      A various field organization of volunteers
      A clandestine group of photographers

Summarising this, it's the story of a volunteer who offered for the "cleanup" mission, to stop the island, code-named "The Piligrim", from reaching international waters and forward the natural reserve. This, whit the help of a newly invented bacteria "Degradobacter Spectraris" which can decompose most types of organic and inorganic waste, invention provided by a scientist and supervised by the chiefs volunteer. The mission seems simple, enter the island, use and place the microoganism inside some key points, leave, but things get complicated when a group of photographers willing to do whatever it takes for a picture that may cause more problems. Compromise the public image of the organization with an unauthorized operation, exposed a secret investigation, and what previously was the concern for the wrold knowing about a trash, its origins, and that its floating through the ocean.
